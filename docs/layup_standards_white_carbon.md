# Layup Standards — White Carbon (v0)

Goal: achieve a “white carbon” aesthetic on a static display model.
This doc starts as a plan; it becomes standards after the first successful layup.

## Open questions
- How “white carbon” will be achieved:
  - pigmented resin?
  - white backing layer?
  - clear coat over carbon + white substrate?
- Layup method:
  - hand layup vs vacuum bagging
- Resin system choice + cure schedule

## First layup test plan (small scale)
- Make a small flat panel test:
  - carbon layer + white backing candidate
  - test finish/clarity and print-through
- Record:
  - weave appearance
  - bubbles/dry spots
  - cure behavior
